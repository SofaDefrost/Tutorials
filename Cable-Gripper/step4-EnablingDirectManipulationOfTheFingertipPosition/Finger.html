Step 4: Direct manipulation of the fingertip position.

In the previous steps, we showed how to model and simulate a soft robot with a finger shape and made of a deformable material (silicone). We also explained how to add a cable to this model to actuate it, and how to add an interactive script to simulate pulling on this cable. Deforming a soft robot in such a way can be considered as an indirect way to control the fingertip position. Indirect because to change the fingertip position ones need to pull the cable as a puppeteer. In this step, we will see how to add a direct control of the fingertip position.


How to use the demo

After clicking on "Animate" you can select the orange sphere (shift + click left) and move it around. The effector, represented with a green square at the fingertip, will follow at best the sphere by acting on the cable.
